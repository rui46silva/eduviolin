FAQ'S

How much does it cost to hire Edu?
My prices vary depending on a number of factors that will be specific to each event, such as type of performance, date, location, length of set(s) etc. The best thing to do is to fill out the form on my CONTACT page, giving as much information as you can so that I get back to you as quickly as possible with an accurate quote.
Do you require a deposit?
Yes. I require 25% of the payment to act as a booking fee to secure the date. The remaining balance then needs to be received in the week before the event.
Where are you based and how far do you travel?
I am based in Porto, Portugal, but travel up and down the country as well as internationally. For some international bookings I may need to travel with a tour manager, depending on the event.
How long do you play for?
Set lengths vary depending on the performance package you would like to book. I can perform anything from a 15-minute show to 2 x 45-minute sets maximum (or equivalent).
What songs do you play, and do you take requests?
For a copy of my current repertoire list, please click here.
Do you take requests?
Of course! Bear in mind, I have to check availability of instrumentals for that song and compatibility to be played on the violin. Also, I need to be informed about these requests as soon as possible, so I can learn them.
Can we choose the set list?
I have ready-made playlists that I know from experience work well for different types of events. However, if there are any genres you would particularly like to focus on, or any songs/pieces that are big no-no’s, please do let me know.
Can you perform with other musicians?
Yes! Although most of my performances are solo, I work with other musicians. If you are interested in a special ensemble, I can give you more information. I work with different DJs, saxophonists, other string players/classical musicians, pianists – just contact me!
Do you provide your own PA/sound equipment?
Yes – depending on the size of the event. For Ambient Music in events up to 150 guests, I can provide my own PA system (Alto TS312). For larger events, impactful performances, and bookings outside Portugal, I require a DJ or AV company at the venue with sound engineer for whom I can provide a specific tech spec.
Can you play in a location without a power outlet?
Yes, I can use a battery powered Speaker (Bose S1), which provides-me flexibility to play in some remote locations. Bear in mind that this sound system is not extremely powerful, and works only for more intimate settings, such as ceremonies or serenades/surprises.
Are you able to perform outside?
Yes – as long as you can provide a performance space that is sheltered from potential rain and direct sunlight - ideally a large parasol or gazebo structure. This shaded area needs to be big enough to cover me and my violin whilst playing, as well as my PA system. I will also require a power socket nearby so that I can plug my PA in there/run an extension lead from there to the performance area.
How long do you need to set up?
I usually aim to arrive between 1 hour – 90 minutes before I am due to start performing. I will need this time to set up any sound equipment and do a quick sound check.
